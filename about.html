About Us
Poco Pastry bakery was established in 2021.
As a Homemade bakery, receiving cake designs on request. Poco Pastry is oriented in a cute, gentle style. We specialize in all kinds of cakes (Fondant Cake, Chesecake, Tiramisu, Cupcake...), and Asian-European desserts.
Our Story
About ingredients and quality: Poco Pastry uses high-quality, safe ingredients, ensuring that the cakes are always fresh when delivered.
Poco Pastry is always improving towards professionalism and perfection in every detail of the cakes.
